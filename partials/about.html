About Me

Hi, I'm Olivia — I usually go by oliiiiiiiii or Olí online. Welcome to my little corner of the web! This is where I share my thoughts, experiences, and things I discover along the way.

I graduated from Yan-Ping High School and am currently studying Computer Science at NTU. This school and major had been my dream since junior high, and even though it can be challenging at times, I still love what I'm learning.

I started this blog to document my life — and, well, a lot of people in my department have beautiful blogs, so I figured... why not have one too?
